== softandfluffynotes

A rails application that manages sets of user notes along with their tagging, and up/down voting.

== Requirements

*   PostgreSQL and the pg gem.  As the app progresses through development, more Postgres specific
    syntax may be used.

*   Bootstrap-sass-rails gem for layout design and css.

*   Unicorn as the app server.

*   Testing through rspec, capybara, and factory_girl_rails gems.   

*   Devise gem for authentication.

=== Misc
*   shoulda, simple_form
